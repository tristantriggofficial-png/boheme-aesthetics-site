Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Who We Are

Bohème Aesthetics ("we," "us," or "our") is a holistic facial spa and skincare boutique located in Palm Desert, California. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or book a service with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, and email address when you reach out to us or book an appointment
  • Booking details — service preferences, appointment history, and any intake information you provide
  • Communications — messages you send us via email, phone, or contact forms
  • Usage data — general information about how you interact with our website (pages visited, browser type, device type) collected automatically via standard web logs

We do not collect payment card information directly. Payments are processed through our third-party booking platform (PocketSuite), which has its own privacy practices.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Communicate with you about your services or inquiries
  • Send appointment reminders or follow-up care instructions
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your information:

  • PocketSuite — online booking and appointment management
  • Google Fonts — web fonts loaded from Google's servers
  • GoHighLevel — customer relationship management and chat widget

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies to support site functionality and improve your browsing experience. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site features may not work as intended.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Client records are typically retained for a period consistent with California business record-keeping requirements.

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
