Her Story

Why she does this work.

Francheska Paula (Franki) is a holistic, research-oriented Licensed Aesthetician based in Palm Desert, CA. She graduated from the International School of Beauty in the Coachella Valley, recognized as a top student under mentors Mirela Marinescu and Lisa Nabb.

With a background in Respiratory Care, graphic art, film, and over 20 years of customer service experience, Franki brings a uniquely layered perspective to her work — equal parts science, artistry, and human connection.

"In an ever-evolving industry, I am always striving to grow and further my education, while never forgetting to reconnect with my roots and relearn the art of slowing down. My goal is to help you feel beautiful in your own skin and age gracefully, just as nature intended."

— Francheska Paula
